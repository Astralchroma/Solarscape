Solarscape (Gateway) v0.0.0

You probably aren't supposed to be here right now (unless you are), but that's not an issue, everything you see here is barebones right now as the goal is to make a technically playable public alpha that we can use as the foundations to continue developing the game, so that's why this page looks straight out of the early 90s.

The database won't be reset prior to the public alpha launch unless something goes wrong and it's easier to just nuke it and start over, so feel free to create your account now and effectively reserve your username.

Username


(0-9A-Za-z_, 1..=32 characters )

Email

While required, Email Addresses aren't currently verified. Once verification is implemented, all accounts will be locked pending Email verification. While you can provide a dummy Email now, you do so risking losing access to your account in the future, no support will be provided.

Password


For the rightfully security conscious, while this is barebones, we have not cut corners on password security, passwords are hashed and salted with Argon2.
